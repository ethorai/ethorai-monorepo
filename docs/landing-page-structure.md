# Landing Page Structure — EU Therapist (v1)

## Purpose

This document defines the **fixed structure** of the landing page generated by the system.

The structure is intentionally constrained to:

- ensure ethical and professional communication
- reduce AI hallucinations
- maintain consistency and trust
- prevent marketing-driven or outcome-based content

The AI must strictly follow this structure.  
Any deviation requires regeneration.

---

## Global Rules

- Calm, neutral, professional tone
- No outcome promises or guarantees
- No sales or urgency language
- Focus on process, not results
- All content must be derived from user inputs

---

## Section 1 — Header / Identity

### Purpose

Establish credibility and professional context.

### Content

- Full name
- Professional role
- Location (city / country, if provided)

### Rules

- Factual information only
- No adjectives or self-promotion

---

## Section 2 — Hero Section (Who the Practice Is For)

### Purpose

Help visitors understand whether the practice may be relevant to them.

### Content

- One short headline
- One explanatory subheadline

### Rules

- Use “I work with…” or equivalent phrasing
- Avoid problem-solving or outcome-oriented language
- Maximum 12 words for the headline

---

## Section 3 — Areas of Support

### Purpose

Provide clarity on topics addressed in the practice.

### Content

- Bullet list of topics

### Rules

- Use neutral, descriptive phrasing
- No medical diagnoses unless explicitly provided
- Do not frame items as problems to be solved

---

## Section 4 — How I Work (Therapeutic Approach)

### Purpose

Explain the therapeutic framework and working style.

### Content

- One short paragraph describing the approach

### Rules

- Describe the process, not results
- Avoid definitive or prescriptive statements
- Maintain a professional and explanatory tone

---

## Section 5 — What You Can Expect

### Purpose

Build trust and reduce uncertainty.

### Content

- 3 to 5 short statements describing the therapeutic environment

### Rules

- Emphasize confidentiality, respect, and collaboration
- Avoid emotional or persuasive language
- No promises of outcomes

---

## Section 6 — Session Formats and Practical Information

### Purpose

Clarify logistical details.

### Content

- Session format (online, in-person, or both)
- Location details if applicable

### Rules

- Factual and concise
- No pricing or promotional language

---

## Section 7 — Contact or Booking

### Purpose

Provide a clear and ethical next step.

### Content

- Short call-to-action text
- Contact or booking method

### Rules

- Neutral CTA phrasing (e.g. “Book a session”, “Contact me”)
- No urgency or persuasive framing
- CTA text limited to 3–4 words

---

## Section 8 — Ethical Disclaimer

### Purpose

Ensure regulatory awareness and trust.

### Content

- Short disclaimer adapted to the professional role

### Rules

- Mandatory inclusion
- Calm and non-alarmist tone
- No legal advice or medical claims

---

## Section 9 — Footer

### Purpose

Complete the page with professional information.

### Content

- Name
- Professional role
- Location
- Contact information
- Placeholder for legal mentions

### Rules

- No additional messaging
- No marketing content

---

## Validation Rules

The system must validate that:

- all sections are present
- section-specific rules are respected
- forbidden language does not appear
- disclaimers are included

If validation fails, the page must be regenerated or corrected before rendering.
